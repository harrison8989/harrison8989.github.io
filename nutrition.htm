Beans are an excellent source of nutrition, with one example being the red bean, which is popular in East Asian cuisines. Here are the nutritional facts for 1 cup of Azuki, or red beans:

Category Quantity Daily Value
Total Fat 1 g 1%
Cholesterol 0 mg 0%
Sodium 10 mg 0%
Potassium 2,470 mg 70%
Total Carbs 124 g 41%
Dietary Fiber 25 g 100%
Protein 39 g 78%

In addition, red beans supply 13% of the DV of Calcium, 54% of the DV of Iron, 35% of the DV of Vitamin B-6, and 62% of the DV of Magnesium. It doesn't hurt that red beans are delicious as well!
