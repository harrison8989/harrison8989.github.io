Hot Pot Night

Spring Quarter

One of our most popular events each year, Hot Pot Night is hosted for the Stanford Community during the Winter Quarter to allow students to understand an integral part of Hong Kong cuisine during the colder months. Hot pot is served for anyone and everyone that is interested in learning more about Hong Kong cuisine and is a great way to make new friends!
