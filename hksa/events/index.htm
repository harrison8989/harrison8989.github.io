Recurring

Dim Sum Runs

Year Round

Dim sum outings are hosted regularly throughout the year! HKSA aims to satisfy your dim sum cravings and will help facilitate transportation.

Welcome Back Lunch

Autumn Quarter

A lunch at the beginning of the academic year to welcome new members and catch up with old faces. Everyone is welcome!

Chinese New Year

Winter Quarter

An annual dinner to celebrate Chinese New Year with the HKSA Community! Join us as we go off-campus for the festivities.

Hot Pot Night

Spring Quarter

One of our most popular events each year, Hot Pot Night is hosted for the Stanford Community during the Winter Quarter to allow students to understand an integral part of Hong Kong cuisine during the colder months. Hot pot is served for anyone and everyone that is interested in learning more about Hong Kong cuisine and is a great way to make new friends!
