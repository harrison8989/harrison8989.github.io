Takero Sone

Email: me@example.com

Position: President
Major: Chemical Engineering
Favorite Dim Sum Dish: Ma lai gao

Hello! My name is Takero Sone and I am a junior majoring in Chemical Engineering with an interest in energy technologies. I was born and raised in Hong Kong and have been immersed in Hong Kong culture for much of my life, whether it be the food, TVB drama, or the fast-paced life of Hong Kong! With my roots in Hong Kong culture, I am glad that I can share this with the Stanford community by leading HKSA this year. My goal is for HKSA to organize events that can promote an understanding and appreciation for Hong Kong culture and current events. I hope to meet you at the exciting events we have planned for the year!
