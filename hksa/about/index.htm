About Us

The Hong Kong Student Association is a group of undergraduate and graduate students interested in Hong Kong and its culture. We hold monthly events celebrating Hong Kong culture and events relating to current affairs in Hong Kong.

Some of the events that we hold each year include:

  • Hot Pot Night
  • Games Night
  • Hike and Dim Sum Run
  • For more details, check out our events page!

We are always excited to have new members, and we hope that you can join us!

Becoming involved with HKSA

Becoming a member is easy! Just sign up for our mailing list and join our Facebook group. If you are interested in taking on leadership roles within the HKSA, there are several opportunities to do so.

Student Interns

Each year we recruit interns to help with planning events. Becoming an intern is a great opportunity to gain leadership experience early into one's undergraduate career at Stanford and to work with like-minded individuals!

Interns need not be freshman but should be ready to delve straight into the HKSA community!

HKSA Core Member

We recruit core members to our team every May for the upcoming school year. New and returning members welcome, as are undergraduate and graduate students!

Stay tuned for more details about the process!
